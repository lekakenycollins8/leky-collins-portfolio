## Overview

Jackier Training & Development (JTD) is a modern coaching and leadership development platform that provides transformative training programs for executives and professionals. Built around the Jackier Method, the website offers structured coaching programs, upcoming event registrations, and insightful blog content to support personal and organizational growth.

## Features
- **The Jackier Method: A proven approach to leadership and coaching, prominently featured throughout the platform.

- **Dynamic Program Pages: Users can explore structured coaching programs across five key categories—Management, Time Recovery, Clarity, Leadership, and Sales.

- **Upcoming Events Showcase: Displays online coaching events with Zoom registration.

- **Responsive and Accessible Design: Fully optimized for all devices, ensuring seamless navigation.

- **Testimonials Integration: Industry experts' endorsements are prominently displayed on the homepage.

- **Contact & Newsletter Sign-Up: Enables users to stay connected with JTD.

Tech Stack
- **Frontend: Next.js & React.js

- **UI Components: shadcn UI

- **Styling: Tailwind CSS

- **Animations: Framer Motion

- **Deployment: Hosted on Vercel
