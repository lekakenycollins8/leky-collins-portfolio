## Overview

KK Law is a modern legal services website that presents a law firm's expertise, team members, and available legal services. The platform offers a clean and professional user experience, making it easy for clients to navigate and connect with the firm.

## Live Demo

[Visit KK Law Website](https://kk-law.vercel.app/)

## Features

- **Modern and Professional Design**: Ensures credibility and trustworthiness.
- **Fully Responsive**: Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- **Detailed Service Descriptions**: Clearly explains legal services offered.
- **Team Member Profiles**: Showcases law firm members and their expertise.
- **Contact Form for Inquiries**: Enables clients to reach out effortlessly.
- **Easy Navigation & User-Friendly Interface**: Ensures smooth browsing.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: React.js & Next.js
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **TypeScript**: Ensures type safety and maintainability
- **Deployment**: Hosted on Vercel
