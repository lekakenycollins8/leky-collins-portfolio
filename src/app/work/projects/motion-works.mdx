## Overview

Motionworks Physiotherapy is a modern, user-friendly website designed to provide essential information about physiotherapy services and facilitate client engagement. The platform ensures easy navigation, appointment scheduling, and accessibility across all devices.

## Live Demo

[Visit Motionworks Physiotherapy Website](https://motionworksphysioclinic.com)

## Features

- **Appointment Booking Integration**: Clients can schedule appointments via call, WhatsApp, or email.
- **Detailed Service Descriptions**: Provides a comprehensive breakdown of physiotherapy treatments.
- **Testimonials Section**: Showcases past client experiences to build trust.
- **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
- **Team Page**: Displays physiotherapists' credentials and expertise.
- **SEO Optimization**: Ensures high visibility on Google search results.
- **User-Friendly Contact Form**: Enables potential clients to reach out effortlessly.
- **Branding & Design Assistance**: Helped in developing a visual identity for the business.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js (React Framework)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **SEO Optimization**: Metadata, structured content, and performance tuning
- **Deployment**: Hosted on Vercel for speed and reliability

## Deployment

Motionworks Physiotherapy is hosted on Vercel, ensuring high performance and seamless uptime.
